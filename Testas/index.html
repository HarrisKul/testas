Client Kickoff

Our digital planing roadmap for inbound begins with a client kickoff meeting. We identify goals and priorities, and lay a foundation that will build a strong agency/client relationship.

Messaging Development

In this phase of the roadmap, we document your company's brand voice/tone remarkables, your ideal customer profile, buyer personas, SEO keywords, and campaign architecture.

Sales Alignment

Marketing and sales must be alignes for our effrorts to be effective, so we dedicate time to form field strategy, lifecycle criteria, account and leading scoring, sales channels, and results reporting.

Campaign Strategy

Now for the fun part. It's time to optimize your digital presence, map out the buyer's journey, and develop campaigns! Once the roadmap is in place, we move to Build & Setup, and a monthly cadence of inbound marketing execution.
